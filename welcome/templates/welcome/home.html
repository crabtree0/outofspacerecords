About Us

Following a lifelong passion for music and a dream of opening a record shop, we finally opened "Out Of Space Records" in 2022. We are based at Kibworth Antiques Centre in Kibworth Harcourt Leicestershire, after the owner's specifically asked for a record seller to be situated there.
This is one of Leicestershire's largest antiques and interiors centre with over 80 dealers. It is an Aladdin's cave, with many rooms of antiques, curios and furniture.
Out of Space Records aims to sell many genres of music, continually increasing and replacing our stock over time and selling them at a reasonable and realistic price, selling both old issues and brand new releases. We also stock music related items such as books, CDs, DVDs, 7 inch singles, 12 inch singles, posters and memorabilia.

If you should have any vinyl or music related items that you no longer need, want or love, please contact us and we will endeavour to offer a good price for your collection, which we will pay cash for.
Please contact us first as we will see you strictly by appointment only. You can call us Monday to Friday 9am to 5pm on 07806 509423 or email us at outofspacerecords@yahoo.com
We are happy to buy most genres of music, however, we would prefer not to buy country and western, musicals, easy listening and Reader's Digest box sets.
We would love you to pay us a visit and whilst here, why not pop into AJ's Coffee shop for tea and cake or a light lunch? After all with music and cake...what more could you want?
